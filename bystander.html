Psychological First Aid Basics

When a peer is overwhelmed by public campus conflict or digital exposure, implement the **Look, Listen, Link** toolkit checklist:

1. Look for Distress Signposts

Watch out for text hyper-defensiveness, sudden radio silence in assignment logs, or avoiding campus tutorial classes entirely.

2. Listen without Fixing Instantly

When they explain their perspective, avoid jumping in with academic judgements. Let them vent safely to process the immediate panic spikes.

3. Link to Low-Barrier Networks

Gently guide them to anonymous chatbots or Senior Allies to safeguard their grading markers or handle group resets officially.
